TATAMI ROOMS

Reservations

Nakato boasts the only private Tatami Rooms, or traditional Tea Rooms, in the Southeast. These rooms offer intimate, multi-course Washoku Dining, where guests feel as though they are being transported to the heart of traditional Japan.

In the Tatami Rooms, guests remove their shoes and enter a serene environment. Throughout the evening, servers attend to their every dining need, as guests sit on floor pillows.

Our Tatami Rooms require a reservation at least 48 hours in advance, as each menu is individually crafted utilizing the freshest fish, produce and other seasonal ingredients, for your ultimate dining pleasure.

Please note: our Tatami Rooms are appropriate for ages 10 and up, as it requires sitting still for a period of time in addition to there being breakables in these rooms.
